Smart contracts form the building block of Aegis. For instance, you as a hotelier may wants to list your room, all you have to do is create a smart contract from our templates and list the smart contract for sale. If a tourist is wants to make a booking for your room, he/she purchases this contract. Once the payment is made the smart contract is run on the blockchain. As soon as this happens it becomes a self-operating, self-enforceable computer program that executes when the programmed conditions are met.
So why would this, as a hotelier or tourist, help you? You could use this to manage the length of booking and stay or perhaps even help mediate escrow based payment agreements between each other.
At the core of the platform the global blockchain ledger that maintains the state of all agreements and smart contracts made through the platform. This is cryptographically secured and kept synchronised in real time across the globe. Just this “real-time, across the globe” capability allows services such as real-time booking, payments and cancellations.
